Website Overview

The Beach Bakery website is a e-commerce website that showcases our bakery goods and lets you place orders online. Use is supported on both desktop and mobile.


Goal of the Website

Our main goal is to expand our business through an easy to navigate website. Convenient features such as online orders and pictrues of our products are available to all potential customers.


Target Audience

  • Local residents
  • Families
  • Students

Important Information

Customers will need easy access to our menu, prices, location, business hours, etc.


Why Shop With Us?

  • Fresh baked goods made daily
  • Affordable prices
  • Family friendly environment
  • Fast service
  • Great customer service
